Chicken Curry

Ingredients:
  • 800g chicken
  • 1/2-1coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves



Steps:
1.Saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant
Add in chicken
Continue to saute until chicken releases oil
2.Add in water,tamrind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar and salt.
3.Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked thoroughly.
4.Add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate.
5.Remove and serve
