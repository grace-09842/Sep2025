About Us

Established in 2010, the School of Computing has grown into a leading center for research, teaching, and innovation. Our faculty comprises experienced professionals and researchers passionate about computer science education.

We emphasize hands-on learning, teamwork, and cutting-edge projects that give students real-world experience and confidence in their future careers.
