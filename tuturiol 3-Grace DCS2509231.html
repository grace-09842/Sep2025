THE WOLF AND THE SHADOW

One evening, a thirsty wolf went to a river to drink water.

As he drank, he saw his reflection in the water.

He thought: "Wow! I look so strong and powerful!"

He flexed his muscles and admired his shadow.

Then he saw what looked like a lion’s reflection nearby!

Frightened, he jumped back and ran away.

Later, he realized it was just a rock and a bush behind him.

He laughed and said: "Next time, I’ll look more carefully".

MORAL: DON’T BE FOOLED BY APPEARANCES.
