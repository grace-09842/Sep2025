Admission & Application

Welcome to the School of Computing! We are excited that you’re considering joining our programmes. Below you’ll find the basic information on how to apply and the requirements for admission.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed secondary education or equivalent qualification.
  • Pass in Mathematics and English.
  • Interest in computing, programming, or information technology.

Application Process

  1. Download and complete the online application form.
  2. Prepare all required documents (transcripts, certificates, ID copy).
  3. Submit your documents through our online portal or email to admissions@gguniversity.edu.my.
  4. Wait for the confirmation email from our admissions team.
